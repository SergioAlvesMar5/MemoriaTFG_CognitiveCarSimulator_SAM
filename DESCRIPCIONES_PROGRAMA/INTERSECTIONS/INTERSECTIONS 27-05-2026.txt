función de BP_Sign_Stop


ComputeStopValidationData:



variables locales:

ForwardSpeedAbs Float

SignedDistanceToStopLine Float

TravelTimeToStopLine Float

DynamicStopWindowRatio Float

AllowedStopBeforeLine Float


inputs:

TargetCar BP_VehicleAi2



outputs:

IsStoppedEnough Boolean

IsInsideStopWindow Boolean

HasWaitedEnough Boolean

ShouldTimeoutAsBadStop Boolean



flujo:


se setea ForwardSpeedAbs como ABS(GetForwardSpeed(TargetCar)).

luego se setea SignedDistanceToStopLine como dot((GetActorLocation(TargetCar) - StopTargetLocationdelAIControllerdeTarget), StopLineForwardVectordelAIControllerdeTarget).
